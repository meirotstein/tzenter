Privacy Policy for Tzenter WhatsApp Bot

Effective Date: Feb 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tzenter! This Privacy Policy explains how our WhatsApp bot operates and how we handle user data. Tzenter is designed to provide services via WhatsApp without storing, collecting, or sharing any personal information.

2. No Data Collection

Tzenter does not collect, store, or process any personal data from users. Our bot functions as a real-time communication tool without retaining any user messages, contacts, or other identifiable information.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no processing or sharing of information. Messages sent through the Tzenter WhatsApp bot are processed in real-time and not stored after they are delivered.

4. Third-Party Services

Tzenter operates solely within WhatsApp and does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data. However, WhatsApp itself may collect and process user data as per its own Privacy Policy. Please refer to WhatsApp’s official privacy policy for more details.

5. Security

As Tzenter does not store any user data unless a user chooses to register a Minyan, in which case their WhatsApp name and phone number are stored for coordination purposes only, there are no data security risks associated with data retention. All communications occur within WhatsApp’s encrypted environment.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services. Any changes will be posted here with the updated effective date.

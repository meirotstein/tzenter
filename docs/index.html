About Tzenter

Tzenter is a non-profit initiative designed to help Jewish individuals arrange a Minyan â€“ a quorum of ten people required for communal prayer.

The service operates via a WhatsApp bot, which simply checks if ten or more people in a given area are interested in davening together.

Why This Service Matters

Many people struggle to find a Minyan, especially in non-central locations. Tzenter provides a simple, automated solution to help them connect.

Privacy and Compliance

Tzenter is completely non-profit, does not store any user data, and exists solely to help facilitate communal prayer.
